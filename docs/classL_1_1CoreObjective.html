Detailed Description

Implements an objective.

Like every core object, core objectives are the essence of what they represent in the sense that contrary to Objective's, it posesses its attributes. CoreObjective's should never be exposed to the user. Only the Environment class shall be allowed for their creation. Environment is in charge of core objective's memory.
